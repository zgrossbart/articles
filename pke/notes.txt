If you're reading this article at Starbucks anyone else can snoop and read it too.  That's fine for an article, but other latte drinkers could grab your Facebook password while you're updating your friends.  That's why <a href="http://mashable.com/2011/01/27/facebook-https/">Facebook supports https</a>.
